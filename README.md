# 🌍 KindNet - Food Donation & Community Support Platform

> Connecting Kindness Through Technology ❤️

KindNet is a web-based platform that connects food donors, NGOs, and people in need through a simple and efficient digital platform. The project aims to reduce food wastage and ensure surplus food reaches communities facing food insecurity.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

Food waste is a major global issue, while many people struggle to access nutritious meals. KindNet addresses this challenge by providing a centralized platform where:

- Donors can contribute surplus food.
- NGOs can manage and distribute donations.
- Receivers can request assistance.
- Communities can collaborate to reduce food waste.

The platform promotes social responsibility and community welfare through technology.
